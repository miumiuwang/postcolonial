Digital Sovereignty and Indigenization

2023

China

Postcolonial computing emphasises the social and political impacts of technology, focusing on the role of technology in social justice, including digital rights, data privacy, and digital sovereignty.

In the digital realm, postcolonial theories can lead to the development of fairer intellectual property laws. China should strengthen digital intellectual property and cultural protection to protect traditional culture and knowledge from plagiarism or misuse. Therefore, it is crucial to emphasise the documentation, preservation, and sustainability of indigenous knowledge in digital form, including text, audio, video, and other forms, and develop appropriate data management and preservation strategies to ensure the long-term protection of knowledge.
